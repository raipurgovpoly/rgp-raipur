WELCOME TO RAIPUR GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE

HISTORY

Raipur Govt. Polytechnic is situated at Siromonipur, which is three (3 KM) from Raipur Block town and 66 kms from Bankura District town. It is situated on the road of Bankura-Jhargram Road via Raipur. Our main thrust is to develop the skill of the student as per the International Industries. Such that they can cope up with heavy Industries, Medium Industries, Small Industries, Service Sector & I.T Sector. Side by side our aims also is to generate new method of thinking in handicraft section of the District such that they can do value addition in this Handicraft industries so that they will be viable in the International market.

OUR COURSES

At present we provide three courses for the diploma degree.

60 Seats for Mechanical Engineering

Vision: The Mechanical Engineering Department should be recognized for outstanding education leading to well-qualified engineers who will be innovative, entrepreneurial and successful in the field of their professional activities.

60 Seats for Electrical Engineering

Vision: To produce quality Electrical engineers by building strong teaching and research environment in the department whose ethical and significant contribution to the society will help socio-economic growth of the locality and the country as a whole.

60 Seats for Civil Engineering

Vision: To produce Civil engineers with outstanding knowledge, skills and attitudes, who can be entrusted by the society to lead a sustainable world with enhanced quality of life.
